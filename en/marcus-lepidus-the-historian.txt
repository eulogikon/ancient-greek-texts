Marcus Lepidus, a Hellenistic historian of the 1st century BCE. Surviving historical works in Greek. Free to read and download from the Greek library archive.

Marcus Lepidus the Historian is not attested in standard scholarly sources for antiquity. The name Marcus Lepidus is associated with the Roman political figure Marcus Aemilius Lepidus, a member of the Second Triumvirate, but he is not recorded as an author of historical works. Major reference works, including the Oxford Research Encyclopedia of Classics, Encyclopædia Britannica, and Smith's Dictionary of Greek and Roman Biography and Mythology, contain no entry for a historian named Marcus Lepidus.

No titles, subjects, or fragments of any historical work attributed to Marcus Lepidus are recorded in the consulted sources. The figure holds no established significance in classical scholarship, as he is not documented in the canonical bibliographies or fragment collections for Greek or Roman historians.
