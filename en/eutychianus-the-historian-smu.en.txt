Eutychianus the Historian, a late antique Greek writer. Historical works in fragmentary transmission. Free to read and download from the Greek library archive.

Eutychianus the Historian lacks any reliable biographical information. The name Eutychianus is associated with other figures, including Pope Eutychianus in the third century and a sixth-century magister officiorum under Emperor Anastasius I, but neither is known as a historian. No historical works are credited to an author of this name in standard scholarly references. The figure of Eutychianus the Historian therefore has no established historical basis. The name likely originates from confusion with other individuals or may refer to a lost or misattributed author not documented in major resources.
