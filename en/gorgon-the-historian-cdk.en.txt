Gorgon the Historian, a 4th to 3rd century BCE Greek historian. Lists his surviving historical works. Free to read and download from the Greek library archive.

Gorgon the Historian was active in the 4th–3rd centuries BCE. No details of his life, including his origins or education, are recorded. He is credited with a single prose work in Koine Greek, but its title, subject, and current status are unknown. Gorgon remains an obscure figure with no documented influence on the subsequent historiographical tradition.
