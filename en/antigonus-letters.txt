Antigonus, a Hellenistic author of the 3rd–2nd century BCE. Surviving epistles of rhetorical interest. Free to read and download from the Greek library archive.

No biographical information exists for an author named Antigonus who wrote rhetorical letters. The name was common in the Hellenistic period, most notably borne by Macedonian kings and generals like Antigonus I Monophthalmus, but these figures are not known as literary epistolographers.
