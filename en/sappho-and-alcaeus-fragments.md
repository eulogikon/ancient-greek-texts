# Fragments of Sappho and Alcaeus

*Σαπφοῦς καὶ Ἀλκαίου ἀποσπάσματα*

**eul_aid:** `anc` · [HTML page](https://eulogikon.org/en/sappho-and-alcaeus-fragments)

Sappho and Alcaeus were major lyric poets from Lesbos. Their surviving works include poetic fragments. Free to read and download from the Greek library archive.
