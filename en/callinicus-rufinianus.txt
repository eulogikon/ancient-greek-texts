Callinicus Rufinianus, Imperial era biographer of the 1st to 4th century CE. Biographical works. Free to read and download from the Greek library archive.

Callinicus Rufinianus is an obscure figure of the Greco-Roman imperial period, as no biographical details are recorded in major reference works. His name combines the Greek "Callinicus" with the Latin cognomen "Rufinianus," suggesting a cultural context of the first to fourth centuries CE.

He is associated with a single biographical work, though its title and content are unattested. The author's historical significance remains undetermined due to the absence of surviving works or reliable testimonia.
